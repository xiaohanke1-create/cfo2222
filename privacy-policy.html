Device Information

  • Your web browser, IP address, and time zone.
  • Information about the cookies on your device.

Details about the web pages you visit, the websites or search terms that referred you, and your interactions on the site.

How We Collect Device Information:

  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device that may include an anonymous unique identifier. For more about cookies and how to disable them, visit allaboutcookies.org.

Web Beacons, Tags, and Pixels: These electronic files help us understand how users navigate the site.
